About Me

I’m a writer, social media junkie, and non-profit communications director. Detail-oriented, dedicated and resourceful, my approach to communication is focused on the audience and the specific needs of the organizations or individuals I work with in new and innovative ways. Especially talented at distilling complex topics into clear communication, I’ve produced content for businesses as far ranging as law firms and dental offices. In addition, I have designed websites using Content Management Systems for various businesses and started a non-profit that helps those with prior convictions expunge their records.
